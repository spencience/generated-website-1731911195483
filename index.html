American Fork Canyon, Utah

Located in the Wasatch Mountains, American Fork Canyon is a stunning natural wonder that offers breathtaking scenery and plenty of opportunities for outdoor recreation.

Recreational Activities

American Fork Canyon offers a variety of recreational activities, including hiking, camping, fishing, and horseback riding.

  • Hiking Trails
  • Camping Sites
  • Fishing Spots
  • Horseback Riding Trails

Plan Your Visit

Before visiting American Fork Canyon, be sure to check the current weather and road conditions.
